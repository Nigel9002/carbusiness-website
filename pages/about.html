About MOTO KENYA

Your Trusted Partner in Quality Pre-Owned Vehicles

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MOTO KENYA has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted dealerships for quality pre-owned vehicles. With a passion for cars and a commitment to excellence, we've helped thousands of customers find their dream vehicles.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide Kenyans with access to reliable, high-quality vehicles at fair prices. Today, we're proud to be a leading name in the automotive industry, known for our transparency, integrity, and customer-first approach.

🚗

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our customers by providing thoroughly inspected, quality pre-owned vehicles backed by transparent service and unwavering integrity.

We believe that everyone deserves to drive a reliable car. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure every vehicle in our inventory meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
