Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying pre-owned vehicles

How do I schedule a test drive? ▼
You can schedule a test drive by clicking the "Test Drive" button on any vehicle page, or by contacting us directly via phone or WhatsApp. We'll arrange a convenient time for you to come in and experience the vehicle firsthand.
What documents do I need to purchase a vehicle? ▼
You'll need a valid Kenyan ID or Passport, a KRA PIN certificate, and proof of income (for financing). For cash purchases, only ID and KRA PIN are required. We'll guide you through the entire process.
Are all vehicles inspected before sale? ▼
Yes! Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a comprehensive 150+ point inspection by our certified mechanics. We check everything from the engine and transmission to the suspension and electrical systems. You'll receive a full inspection report with every purchase.
Do you offer financing options? ▼
Yes, we partner with leading financial institutions to offer competitive financing options. You can use our online financing calculator to estimate your monthly payments, or speak with our finance team for personalized assistance.
What warranty options are available? ▼
We offer several warranty options ranging from 3-month to 12-month coverage. Our warranties cover engine, transmission, and major electrical components. You can choose the plan that best fits your needs and budget.
Can I trade in my current vehicle? ▼
Absolutely! We offer competitive trade-in values for your current vehicle. Simply visit our Trade-In page or contact us for a free valuation. The trade-in value can be used as a down payment for your new vehicle.
Do you provide vehicle history reports? ▼
Yes, every vehicle comes with a comprehensive history report including service records, accident history, and ownership details. We believe in full transparency so you can make an informed decision.
What is the minimum down payment required? ▼
The minimum down payment typically ranges from 10-20% of the vehicle price, depending on the financing institution and your credit profile. Our finance team will help you find the best options for your situation.
Are warranty claims easy to process? ▼
Yes, we have a streamlined warranty claims process. Simply contact our service department, and we'll arrange for an inspection and repairs at our partnered garages. We're committed to making the process as hassle-free as possible.
